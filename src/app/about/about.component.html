The Aims and Objectives of our forum:

  1. Namma Bidar Welfare Association is a non-profit, non-govt association.
  2. Our Main objective is to Bring all Bidar people residing outside Bidar district under one platform and helping each other.
  3. Another main agenda is contributing back to our motherland Bidar overall Development.
  4. To create a sense of brotherhood, love and affection, among the members of our Association.
  5. To under take various social service activities for the overall development of our Bidar District.
  6. Our Aim is to provide meaningful education that brings human values in children.
  7. Development of Health Programmes.
  8. To focus on the quality education for Bidar children .
  9. To offer mutual support and provide a forum for discussion.
  10. To take up effective, reasonable and lawful steps for the solution of problems relating to members of our Forum or relating to Bidar District.
  11. To undertake and participate in programmes pertaining to health, education, employment
  12. To conduct and or participate in seminars, camps, conferences, research and training programmes.
  13. To organize social activities and cultural programmes for the members and their families.
  14. To exchange views, information, data between the members.
  15. To communicate and participate in different developmental schemes of Central and State Governments
  16. To undertake and assist agricultural development, irrigation and waste & dry land development;
  17. To render help to the needy, deserving or distressed members and or their dependents.
  18. To organize sports events for members and or other cultural, literary, social events for the benefit of the members of the Association
  19. To encourage and implement projects for developing amenities and clean living environment and other such activities to improve the quality of life and well being of the Bidar District.
  20. To improve the livelihoods of rural poor through efficient management of natural resources and increased productivity, profitability and diversity of the farming systems.
  21. To facilitate agro processing, value addition and market linkages for enhanced on farm and off-farm income and employment generation.
  22. To undertake environmental protection activities viz. afforestation, wild life preservation of our Bidar.
